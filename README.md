# CI Unity 📱

**CI Unity** is an education and career development platform designed to help students build practical skills, work on real-world projects, and connect with mentors and a learning community.

The platform focuses on helping students **build an independent career before graduation** through practical learning, coaching, and collaboration.

## 🚀 Features

- 🎓 **Practical Skill Learning**  
  Learn **18+ industry-relevant skills** through hands-on learning modules.

- 🧠 **Project-Based Learning**  
  Work on **45+ real-world projects** to build a strong portfolio.

- 👨‍🏫 **Mentorship & Coaching**  
  Get guidance from experienced mentors and industry experts.

- 🤝 **Community Network**  
  Connect with a national community of students and innovators.

- 🏆 **Competitions & Recognition**  
  Participate in competitions and win awards and prizes.

- 💡 **Innovation Environment**  
  Build and test your ideas with guidance and resources.

- 🎓 **Scholarships & Opportunities**  
  Access scholarships, grants, and growth opportunities.
